Our Heritage

Born in the city that clothed a civilization.

Ile-Ife is regarded by the Yoruba as the source of creation itself — the city where bronze and terracotta casters once shaped the faces of kings. Imperial Outfits carries that same discipline into every Agbada, gele and ceremonial set we cut.

Our tailors train under Ile-Ife's senior craftsmen, combining traditional hand-embroidery with modern silhouettes so every piece feels ancestral and current at once.
